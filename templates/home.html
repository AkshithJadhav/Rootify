Your Personalized Guide to the world of plants

Rootify is an innovative web application designed for seamless plant identification, exploration, and discovery. Built with Python and Flask, it uses AI-powered analysis, real-time chat, and interactive APIs for an engaging user experience. Rootify enables users to identify plants from images, explore categorized species, and discover regional plants via an interactive map. It aims to transform the way users connect with and learn about the world of plants.

Key Features

Rootify combines cutting-edge AI technology with intuitive design to revolutionize your plant identification and exploration journey.

AI-Powered Plant Identification

Upload images of plants for instant analysis through our advanced AI system. Get detailed insights about species, characteristics, and care requirements. Engage with our interactive chat feature powered by Gemini API for real-time plant-related queries.

Comprehensive Plant Categories

Explore our extensive collection of plant species organized into intuitive categories. Browse through detailed plant profiles, discover common names, and access high-quality images. All data is powered by the Trefle API for accurate and up-to-date information.

Interactive Regional Discovery

Navigate through our interactive Indian map to discover plants native to specific regions. Click on any area to view local plant species, powered by the iNaturalist API. Perfect for understanding regional biodiversity and finding plants suited to your location.

Real-Time AI Interaction

Engage in dynamic conversations about plants through our intelligent chat system. Get instant answers to your questions, care tips, and personalized recommendations for your plant journey.
